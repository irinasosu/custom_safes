Wall Safes


Wall safes provide hotels with a unique alternative to the standard guest room safe. Our portfolio of wall safes includes both surface mount and in wall designs and each is available in 15” and 17” laptop sizes.


W 5.0 In-Wall Safe

The W 5.0 in-wall safe is often located in the hotel room closet for guests to easily secure 15″ laptops and more. Designed to install between standard 16″ on-center studs, the W 5.0 occupies the cavity inside the wall while the built-in flange covers rough cuts to provide a finished look.

WX 5.0 Electronic In-Wall Safe

Safemark’s WX 5.0 in-wall safe provides hotel guests with peace of mind while maintaining a small footprint in your closet or guest room. Designed to install between standard 16″ on-center studs, the WX 5.0 will occupy the cavity inside the guest room wall. The safe provides ample space for guests to secure their 17” laptops plus cameras, purses and other valuables. With a clean installation in mind, our built-in flange will cover rough cuts and eliminate unnecessary re-plastering.

SX 5.0 Electronic Surface Mount Wall Safe

The SX 5.0 was designed for hotels requiring wall safe large enough to accommodate a 17” laptop.
